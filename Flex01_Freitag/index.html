  • “HOLACRACY”: OUR ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

    In 2016, FREITAG adopted the Holacracy organizational structure.
    In a nutshell, it means that we distribute authority across the company
    in the form of roles rather than putting it in the hands of a few supervisors and managers.
